Tag mentions of gene entities in text using the AbGene Tagger. This tagger was trained on Medline abstracts, and performance may vary on other types of input.

For full details, see Tanabe and Wilbur (2002) "Tagging gene and protein names in biomedical text", Bioinformatics 18(8):1124&endash;1132, doi:10.1093/bioinformatics/18.8.1124.
