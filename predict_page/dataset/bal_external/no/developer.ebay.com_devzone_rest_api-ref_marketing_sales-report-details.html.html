Promotion Summary Report Details

The following shows two promotions that both run from 9/5 to 9/10 and shows how the baseSale , promotionSale , and precentageSalesLift values are calculated for these promotions.

Promotion 1: Buy 1, Get 1 50% Off

Item Price Sale date Qty sold Total revenue
Item 1 $2 9/1 1 $2
Item 2 $2 9/6 1 $2
Item 3 $2 9/10 2 $1.50
Item 4 $12 9/10 1 $12

Promotion 2: Spend $10, Get 1 $5 Off

Item Price Sale date Qty sold Total revenue
Item 5 $6 9/2 3 $18
Item 6 $6 9/5 3 $13
Item 7 $6 9/9 1 $6

Base Sales Results

The Base sales is $20.00. The baseSale value is the total revenue from all the items that did not trigger the promotion during the promotion period.

Item Price Sale date Qty sold Total revenue Notes
Item 1 $2 9/1 1 $2 Not counted - out of period
Item 2 $2 9/6 1 $2
Item 3 $2 9/10 2 $1.50 Not counted - triggered promotion
Item 4 $12 9/10 1 $12
Item 5 $6 9/2 3 $18 Not counted - out of period
Item 6 $6 9/5 3 $13 Not counted - triggered promotion
Item 7 $6 9/9 1 $6
$20.00

Promotion Sale Results

The promotion sale is $14.50. The promotionSale value is the total revenue from all the items that triggered the promotion during the promotion period.

Item Price Sale date Qty sold Total revenue Notes
Item 1 $2 9/1 1 $2 Not counted - out of period
Item 2 $2 9/6 1 $2 Not counted - did not trigger promotion
Item 3 $2 9/10 2 $1.50
Item 4 $12 9/10 1 $12 Not counted - did not trigger promotion
Item 5 $6 9/2 3 $18 Not counted - out of period
Item 6 $6 9/5 3 $13
Item 7 $6 9/9 1 $6 Not counted - did not trigger promotion
$14.50

Promotion Sale Lift

The precentageSalesLift represents the percentage of the total dollar amount gained due to promotions, which is calculated as follows.

precentageSalesLift = promotionSale / ( baseSale + promotionSale )

In this example, the precentageSalesLift is 42%.

$14.50 / ($14.50 + $20.00) = 42.03%
